  MODULE: Age  (Dative pronoun + number + metai/metų)
======================================================================

  Pattern: <Pronoun dat.> <collective/cardinal number> <metai|metų>.
  Numbers with "metai" use COLLECTIVE numerals (dveji, treji, ketveri …),
  not cardinals. Numbers with "metų" use regular cardinals.


--- Pronouns used ---

  I am         → Man
  You are      → Tau
  He is        → Jam
  She is       → Jai

--- Year form (metai vs metų) ---

  metai — numbers 1-9, compounds ending 1-9 (21, 32, …) → collective numerals
  metų  — numbers 10-20, decades (30, 40, …) → cardinal numerals

--- Collective numerals (used with plurale tantum "metai") ---

  1 → vieneri    2 → dveji      3 → treji      4 → ketveri
  5 → penkeri    6 → šešeri     7 → septyneri   8 → aštuoneri   9 → devyneri


--- Full age expressions (one pronoun per number, rotating) ---
